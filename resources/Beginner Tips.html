Beginner Tips

Why does my the size of my font change in different files despite using the same tool?

Fontsize is tied to the DPI of your files. I highly recommend figuring something out to scale the DPI of your files to their height.

Why are my files so big?

You're working with manga, always export your pages in grayscale. It'll cut your filesize by two thirds. But you should, of course, export colour pages in full colour.
After that, it's also advisable to run your files through a lossles optimizer like Pingo (commandline) / Pinga (GUI).

But never — NEVER — export as jpg.

What can I do about JPG artifacts in my raws?

You can run them through AI models specifically designed to remove them!
Just follow this guide and try some of the jpg models in the database.
If you can't be bothered to set this up on your PC, have a look at the Google Colab section.
