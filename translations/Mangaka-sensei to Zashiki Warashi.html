Mangaka-sensei to Zashiki Warashi

Mangaka-sensei to Zashiki Warashi
マンガ家先生と座敷わらし
AuthorNukku (ぬっく)
Blog booth.pm Fanbox Twitter Pixiv
Official Amazon BookWalker Kobo
PublicationCompleted
TranslationCompleted
Updated17 March 2024
Frequency86 Days

A mangaka moves into a new apartment, and for some reason he can see the zashiki warashi that lives there, but he can’t hear her voice.
Fearing that she’d disappear if she finds out, he pretends that he doesn’t see her, while she continues helping him out with the household.

Publication of the manga has ended, but Nukku is still independently releasing extra chapters on his booth.pm page, continuing the story.
They’re digital, and easily accessible outside of Japan. Please buy them.
