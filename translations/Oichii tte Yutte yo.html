A thorny rich girl who can’t make friends due to her horrible attitude has her blood sucked by an aloof vampire — but her blood is so bad that the vampire decides to help her get closer to her crush, trying to improve the taste of her foul blood even just a little.
What’s going to come of their cooperation?
