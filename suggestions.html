Suggestions

neCo is currently accepting suggestions for a new scanlation project

Below is a list of manga I know of that I could see myself working on.
Please feel free to contact me with your opinion on the below manga, or with any other suggestions you might have.

Generally, I enjoy lolibaba romances, but anything with a cute enough loli will be taken into consideration.
Something between 15 and 20 pages per chapter would be ideal, but this is not a hard requirement.

Considering

Out of the manga I've been considering working on, the following three are the most interesting to me.

Hakuchizu no Reisende [Ongoing, 10 Chapters]

In the darkness, demons can possess living things and turn them into monsters. The darkness thrives in the uncertain, so it can be driven back by clearly defining an area — by mapping it. Rita is a moth loli who uses active sonar to map her surroundings at blinding speeds. Together with a young Pioneer (and some extras), she sets out to discover the mysteries of the darkness.

My personal favourite of the bunch, I think. But as it's a new series with a relatively broad appeal, there's always the chance someone gets to it before I'm done with box goblin.
Also, the more I think about it, the more I'm afraid of getting stuck with a 100+ chapter unending epic that will haunt me till the end of my days. This madman is pumping out 20+ page chapters every week. And I mean, they're good so far, but I can't keep up with that.

Kami-sama no Youjinbou [Ongoing, 4 Chapters]

Samurai is killed in battle. Years after the war, he is reincarnated as a servant of the very fluffy loli kami of the night and moon, Tsukuyomi no Mikoto. He is to exorzise "Kenomono", creatures that amplify people's negative emotions, to cleanse his blood-stained soul before he can be reincarnated as... a rabbit?

Problem with this one is, she's only a loli because she's been split from her main shrine, so she might grow into a hag at some point. I'm scared of that possibility.
Again, pretty new manga. And apparently it's a light novel adaptation.

Ningyou no Asa [Hiatus, 10 Chapters]

A "love doll" escapes from a lab and decides to live with the hikikomori wannabe light novel author who found her one night. Also, there's a talking severed head.

It's weird, unsurprisingly a little lewd, and pretty cute at times. I'm tempted to do this one just because it's only 10 chapters, and I doubt anyone else ever would.

Maybe?

Some more manga I've enjoyed quite a bit, but for whatever reason I feel less motivated about working on them.

Hebigami Kagura! [Completed, 17 Chapters]

Snake kami gets fucked up bad and kidnapped, so the town she governs goes to shit. Her disciple, a grown man who hasn't socially developed past the level of a grade schooler, saves her. She gets a loli body, but has lost most of her power. They now aim to clean up the town and make her a true kami again.

It's very shounen-ish, but kind of in a self-aware way? Slightly? I think that's what it's going for? It doesn't quite hit home with me. Also, the author doesn't draw loli nipples, but has no problem with hag tits, so I don't like him. And going by a cursatory glance at his twitter, he might be a furry too.

Shinyuu no Musume ni Semarare Komatteimasu [Ongoing, 31 Chapters]

Our average corporate slave protagonist suddenly has to look after his friend's daughter, and she's hell-bent on becoming his wife. It doesn't go full drama and EVERYTHING'S ON FIRE like most manga with similar premises I've read, which is nice.

I like it so far. The loli's very territorial. Problem with manga like this is, there's always a very real chance the author's just gonna pull out in the end, and that shit's infuriating to me.

Chinchin Kemokemo [Completed, 19 Chapters]

Miko loli gets easily possessed by animals, usually ends up in some state of undress, and her childhood friend™ then has to calm her down, usually by hugging her or something.

It's cute and lewd. You can very clearly tell when it got axed though. This one technically has a group working on it, but they release about once a year.
Fun fact: The group that originally picked this up was so upset that people kept tagging this romance manga as romance that they abandoned mangadex altogether.

Akuyome [Completed, 40 Chapters]

Guy is lonely, so he does the only reasonable thing and summons a demon from hell to be his wife. Without even looking up, he proposes to it the second the summoning ritual is completed — but it's an absolute abomination.
No worries though, it can turn into a cute loli.

It's surprisingly interesting, and the humour's alright. But while this is technically a lolibaba wife story, it's just not as nice and fluffy as box goblin, aeka and co.




Chapter counts for ongoing manga as of 2023-02-02.
